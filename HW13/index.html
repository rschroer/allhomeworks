D3Times

Correlations Discovered Between Health Risks and Age, Income

My work explores the relationship between emerging class identity and emotional memories. With influences as diverse as Machiavelli and John Lennon, new synergies are crafted from both explicit and implicit layers.

Ever since I was a student I have been fascinated by the theoretical limits of the universe. What starts out as yearning soon becomes corroded into a cacophony of lust, leaving only a sense of what could have been and the dawn of a new order.

As momentary phenomena become clarified through diligent and personal practice, the viewer is left with a tribute to the limits of our era.
